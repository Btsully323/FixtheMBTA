About this website

Why was this site made?

  • -  To fix the MBTA
  • -  Inform the public
  • -  To add on to its infrastructure

FixtheMBTA.com was made as a way to bring general awareness to an issue that plagues not only Boston, but the greater Boston area as a whole.

What was your inspiration for making this site?

My idea for this site came about when I was browsing the internet and found a website called, "https://www.fixmbta.com/". I noticed that the website was not updated and was made on the free website making service, wix.com. I wanted to do my own take on that site with my own views and solutions.
